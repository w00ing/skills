# Chinese Humanization Checklist

Use this checklist after reading the source copy. The goal is natural Mainland Chinese product writing, not decorative rewriting.

## Translationese Patterns

Fix literal English structures:

- `通过……来……` overuse.
- `旨在帮助用户……` when a direct verb is clearer.
- `无缝地`, `高效地`, `轻松地` stacked without proof.
- `不仅……而且……`, `不只是……更是……` used as a default contrast.
- `为……提供了一个……的解决方案`.
- `使得用户能够……`.
- `在……方面` when a concrete verb would do.
- `满足您的……需求` when the need can be named directly.

Prefer direct phrasing:

- `用 Shotomatic 定时截取 Mac 屏幕。`
- `把一组截图导出为 PDF、ZIP 或 MP4。`
- `文件保留在本机，不需要上传到云端。`

## AI-Sounding Product Copy

Flag and rewrite:

- inflated adjectives: `强大`, `极致`, `卓越`, `革命性`, `领先`, `专业级`
- generic outcomes: `提升效率`, `提高生产力`, `优化工作流`
- slogan filler: `一站式`, `全方位`, `智能化`, `赋能`, `打造`
- vague trust claims: `安全可靠`, `保护隐私`, `值得信赖` without mechanism
- repeated triplets: `快速、简单、高效`, `清晰、稳定、可靠`

Replace with evidence:

- `截图在本机生成和导出。`
- `可以按固定间隔重复截图。`
- `可以把同一批截图整理成 PDF 或 ZIP。`

## SEO Stuffing

Chinese SEO terms can appear, but prose must still read like copy for people.

Use target terms naturally:

- `Mac 自动截图工具`
- `自动化截图工具`
- `定时截图 Mac`
- `批量网页截图`
- `截图转 PDF`
- `本地处理`
- `隐私`

Avoid heading/title bundles:

- `Mac 自动截图工具｜自动化截图工具｜定时截图 Mac`
- `批量网页截图、截图转 PDF、本地处理隐私保护`

## Product Truth

Before finalizing, check every claim:

- Does the copy imply Windows, iOS, Android, web app, or cloud sync?
- Does it imply team collaboration or enterprise management?
- Does it imply files are never read by any system when the actual flow is more nuanced?
- Does it promise support for Chinese payment methods, China hosting, ICP filing, or Mainland availability?
- Does it imply Shotomatic can bypass website restrictions, CAPTCHAs, paywalls, or anti-bot systems?

If uncertain, make the claim narrower.

## Mainland Chinese Tone

Prefer:

- practical, calm, specific language
- verbs over nouns
- user workflow before product praise
- short paragraphs
- natural CTAs

Avoid:

- Taiwan/Hong Kong vocabulary
- internet slang
- overly cute tone
- official-bureaucratic phrasing unless legal/support copy requires it
- direct references to piracy, crack sites, or "China traffic" in public copy

## CTA Patterns

Good CTA directions:

- `下载 Shotomatic`
- `开始自动截图`
- `查看价格`
- `试用 Mac 版`
- `了解截图转 PDF`

Avoid vague CTAs:

- `立即体验`
- `探索更多`
- `开启高效之旅`
- `解锁强大功能`

## Self-Check

Before returning final copy:

1. Read it aloud in Chinese.
2. Check that every sentence could plausibly appear on a Mainland Chinese product site.
3. Remove one filler adjective if the sentence still works.
4. Confirm Chinese-English spacing and punctuation.
5. Confirm Shotomatic product truth.
6. Confirm public copy does not mention internal traffic, SEO, analytics, or localization experiments.
